Excursions

Private boat outings from Herron Island for up to four guests. Bring your own food and drinks, enjoy the ride, and let Cap'n Pete handle the water.

Evening Sunset Cruise
$100

1 hour

Slow down and watch the sun settle over Case Inlet.

A simple, beautiful evening ride close to Herron Island. Great for unwinding, taking photos, and enjoying the water without committing to a longer outing.

Bring

  • Warm layer or jacket
  • Camera or phone
  • Drinks or snacks if desired
  • Non-slip shoes
Outing to Warm Beach
$150

2 hours

Beach time, swimming, and a little exploring.

About 30 minutes round trip by boat, plus time at Warm Beach and/or hiking in Haley Park.

Bring

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Beach layers
  • Lunch, snacks, or drinks
  • Small day pack
Trip to Allyn
$200

2.5 hours

Boat over for dinner, dessert, or a casual evening out.

The round trip is about 40 minutes, plus time in Allyn. Pizza, seafood, burgers, and ice cream are within walking distance of the boat dock.

Bring

  • Layers for the ride home
  • Wallet
  • Comfortable shoes
  • Optional drinks for the boat ride
Hiking Adventures
$150

2 hours • $50/additional hour

Make the boat ride part of a bigger outdoor day.

Nearby hiking adventure options include Harstine Island, Warm Beach, McMicken Island, and Joemma Beach. Final destination depends on weather, tides, timing, and dock/beach access.

Bring

  • Hiking shoes
  • Water bottle
  • Day pack
  • Weather-appropriate layers
  • Snacks or picnic food
