Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful details before you book.

How many guests can come?

Trips are limited to up to four guests.

Are life jackets provided?

Yes. Life jackets are provided for all guests.

Can we bring food and drinks?

Yes. Guests are welcome to bring food and drinks, including alcoholic or non-alcoholic beverages, and a cooler with ice.

Is there a restroom on the boat?

No. Please plan accordingly before boarding.

What if the weather changes?

Safety comes first. Trips may be rescheduled or adjusted based on wind, water, visibility, tides, or other conditions. If a trip needs to be cancelled, a full refund will be issued.

Do we need to sign a waiver?

Yes. All guests must review and sign the waiver before boarding.

Is gratuity expected?

Gratuity is appreciated but never expected.

How do we reserve?

Use the reservation request page, email info@capnpete.com, or phone/text 253-222-9835.
