Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful details before you book.

How many guests can come?

Trips are limited to up to four guests.

Are life jackets provided?

Yes. Life jackets are provided for all guests.

Is there a restroom on the boat?

No. Please plan accordingly before boarding.

Do we need to sign a waiver?

Yes. All guests must review and sign the waiver before boarding.

Can we bring food and drinks?

Yes. Guests are welcome to bring food and drinks, including alcoholic or non-alcoholic beverages.

How do we reserve?

Use the reservation request page, email info@capnpete.com, or phone/text 253-222-9835.

What is your cancellation policy?

Your safety and enjoyment are our top priorities.

  • If we cancel due to inclement weather, mechanical issues, or other safety concerns, you'll receive a full refund or may choose to reschedule for another available date.
  • If you cancel at least 48 hours before your excursion, you'll receive a full refund.
  • If you cancel within 48 hours of your scheduled departure, your payment is generally non-refundable. However, if we're able to rebook your reservation, we'll gladly issue a refund or help you reschedule.
  • We understand that unexpected situations arise, so if something comes up, please contact us as soon as possible. We'll always do our best to be fair and work with you.

How do I pay for my excursion?

After you submit a reservation request, we'll confirm availability and send you a secure PayPal payment link. You can pay with your PayPal account or with most major credit or debit cards, even if you don't have a PayPal account. Your reservation is confirmed once payment has been received.

Is gratuity expected?

Gratuity is appreciated but never expected.
